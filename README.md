‚ãÜ Finding out the 5 most frequently featured actors on Netflix <br>

## ‚äπ | Key Findings<br>
‚Å∫ TV-MA content constitutes the largest share among all content ratings on Netflix. <br>
‚Å∫ Rajiv Chilaka is the most frequently featured director on Netflix with a total content count of 22. <br>
‚Å∫ Anupam Kher is the most frequently featured actor on Netflix with a content count of 43. <br>
‚Å∫ From 2015 to 2020, there has been a significant increase in Movies available on Netflix. <br>
